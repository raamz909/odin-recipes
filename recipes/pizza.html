Fior di latte pizza recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (preferably homemade or high-quality store-bought)
  • 1 cup Fior di Latte mozzarella, sliced or torn into pieces
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Semolina flour or cornmeal, for dusting

Preparation

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to its highest setting (450-500°F or 230-260°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. Prepare Dough: On a lightly floured surface, stretch or roll out your pizza dough to about 12 inches in diameter. Transfer it to a piece of parchment paper dusted with semolina flour or cornmeal to prevent sticking.
  3. Add Toppings: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Distribute the Fior di Latte mozzarella over the sauce. Drizzle a little extra virgin olive oil over the top and season with a pinch of salt.
  4. Bake: Carefully transfer the pizza (with the parchment paper) onto the preheated pizza stone or a baking sheet. Bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and crispy, and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Garnish and Serve: Once out of the oven, immediately garnish with fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with a little more extra virgin olive oil if desired. Slice and serve hot.
